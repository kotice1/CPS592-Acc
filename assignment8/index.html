About this app

This is a calculator application. It can be used by clicking the buttons with the mouse or following the instruction below to use your keyboard.

  • Use the 'w', 'x', 'a', 'd' keys to navigate the calculator buttons. When the desired key is highlighted, use the 'f' key to select that value. This is the 5-key application.
  • The 3-key application works the same way and uses the 'j' and 'l' keys to navigate left and right and the 'h' key to select.


  • The following are the trials for the 5-key implementation:

    • Hand: 2 min 34 sec | 2 min 22 sec | 2 min 6 sec | Average: 2 min 21 sec | 2 errors

    Foot: 3 min 43 sec | 3 min 15 sec | 3 min 31 sec | Average: 3 min 30 sec | 5 errors


  • The following are the trials for the 3-key implementation:

    • Hand: 2 min 41 sec | 2 min 32 sec | 2 min 17 sec | Average: 2 min 30 sec | 1 error

    Foot: 3 min 19 sec | 2 min 57 sec | 3 min 4 sec | Average: 3 min 7 sec | 2 errors


  • Summary:

    • These tests that were run are based on the amount of time it takes to input the following 5 equations into the calculator:

  • 4+3*7=25
  • 7*7-4*3=37
  • 8*9/2+3=39
  • 17+22-77/11=32
  • 14+16*3/6=22

    • In general, I noticed that the hand was faster than the foot and the foot made more errors throughout the trials. Additionally, the hand seemed to be faster when there were more possible directions to move in, while the foot would move slower when there were more possible directions (i.e. more keys to push).
